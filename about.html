About

Hello! I'm Eton, a creative professional passionate about music, animation, photography, and product design. I graduated from Harvard in 2024 where I studied Statistics and Economics and am currently working as a product manager at a fintech company.

In 7th grade, I began studying Classical Voice and Opera at the San Francisco Conservatory of Music (Pre-college) and I went on to serve as the General Manager of the Harvard Krokodiloes (Harvard's oldest acapella group) in college. I also further developed my interest in animation and photography when I took my first few courses in AFVS (Department of Art, Film, and Visual Studies) at Harvard. Now working full time in New York, I am part of a funk band with some college friends often performing at local bars in the lower east side and have begun my portfolio in animation and photography on the side. I have also made the career pivot from finance into product to return to my love of creating.

I hope to explore the intersection of music, art, and science and to work with other like minded individuals. This portfolio showcases my journey and achievements. Feel free to explore the different sections to learn more about my work!
